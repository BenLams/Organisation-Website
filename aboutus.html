Our Location

We are in Kinshasa, the capital city of the Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). Kinshasa has a rich history and is known for its vibrant culture and bustling atmosphere. It is situated on the banks of the Congo River, which plays a significant role in the city's transportation and trade. Kinshasa has witnessed various historical events, including the colonial era under Belgian rule and the struggle for independence. Today, it stands as a vibrant and diverse city.
